Understanding Google Dorking

What is Google Dorking?

Google dorking uses advanced search operators to find information that isn't easily accessible through normal searches. These specialized queries can uncover sensitive files, exposed directories, and specific content types.

How Dorks Work

Dorks combine search operators like site:, filetype:, intitle:, and inurl: to narrow down search results. This allows investigators to find specific types of content or information about targets efficiently.

Common Operators

site: Search within specific domains
filetype: Find specific file formats
intitle: Search in page titles
inurl: Search in URLs
